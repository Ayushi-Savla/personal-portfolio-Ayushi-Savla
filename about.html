About Me

A brief introduction about myself, my background, skills, and passions.

My Background

I was born and raised in Nairobi, Kenya, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Strathmore University. My academic journey has shaped my interest in using technology to solve real-world problems, particularly in the areas of sustainability, research, and social impact. I enjoy sharing my experiences with others in the same field to inspire and guide them as they grow.

My Technical Skills

Over time, I have gained practical experience in programming, web and mobile development, and system design. I am proficient in Python, Java, and C++, and I have also worked with IoT technologies in building sensor-based solutions such as a water quality monitoring system. Beyond coding, I have developed skills in event management, research collaboration, leadership, and mentoring, which help me bridge both technical and organizational aspects of projects.

My Interests and Goals

Outside academics, I am passionate about sports, design, and community engagement. I have played competitive tennis, serving as co-captain of the Strathmore University Tennis Team, and I also enjoy swimming and creative design work. My long-term career goal is to integrate technology with sustainable development projects, particularly in water governance and environmental conservation, to create solutions that positively impact both communities and ecosystems.
